Standards Assuring Our Quality

Exceeding Your Expectations

IQNET (The International Certification Network) association is an international association of certification bodies based in Switzerland. With the certificates issued by IQNET, Global Industrial Solutions has an exclusive tool that enables the affirmation of the contribution in promoting quality when providing its services.

By certifying the quality management system, our organization recognizes and can meet the needs and expectations of its users, interested parties, employees, suppliers, and the social community, and gains an advantage over the competition in the most efficient way possible.

By certifying the environmental management system through the implementation of appropriate goals and policies, we effectively and efficiently manage all risks significant from the ecological aspects of the environment. We use a logical and objective methodology to evaluate environmental aspects, based on the importance of their environmental impacts. We have adapted our business system to minimize significant impacts on the environment.

With the growing awareness of the importance of human resources within our organization and their contribution to the value of the organization, the awareness of the need for occupational health and safety management is also growing. By certifying the occupational health and safety management system, we confirm that our organization identifies all risks, determines the level of risks that may occur, manages them and implements constant measures to eliminate them and reduce their impact.
